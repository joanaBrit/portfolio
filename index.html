About Me

Say Hello!

Hi! I'm Joana, originally from Madeira Island, Portugal.I consider myself a positive and resilient person who thrives on challenges and loves to learn. In 2023, I made a significant career transition from fashion to technology. I enrolled in a bootcamp where I gained hands-on experience in developing full-stack applications, learning various technologies, and collaborating within teams. It was during this bootcamp that my passion for technology began.

Driven by excitement and a thirst for continuous learning, I started on a project using TypeScript and Material UI. Additionally, I actively seek opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills by attending events and seeking advice from others. It was through these experiences that I discovered Women Who Code London, where I volunteered and began my journey in a mentorship program, which has now transitioned to the Women Coding Community.

I'm passionate about integrating technical and visual elements together to create meaningful user experiences. The process of creating, learning, and exploring this intersection truly excites me. With a background in design, I offer a different perspective to my work. I'm eager to combine both worlds and put my skills into action.
