About Me

Say Hello!

Hi! I'm Joana, and I'm currently looking for my first role in tech as a Software Engineer. I'm positive, resilient, and passionate about learning, solving problems, and tackling challenges.

In 2023, I transitioned from fashion to tech and completed a Software Engineering Immersive bootcamp at General Assembly, where I learned to build full-stack applications. My curiosity didn't stop there. I began by contributing to open-source projects with the Women Coding Community where I now work as an Evangelist.

I love staying active in tech. Recently, I built a personal project using TypeScript, Material UI, where I also use to learn testing with Jest. I completed JavaScript and Python MOOC Sprint courses, and I'm currently taking an 8-week Python & SQL course with Code First Girls. I'm also part of the Cajigo 100 Women in Tech Accelerator Programme.

"Do it scared" is my motto. I was a speaker at the Halfstack 2024 event with Women Coding Community and have spoken at other online events since. I also participated in my first hackathon with Huddlehive, where I embraced stepping out of my comfort zone.

My background in design brings a creative edge to my engineering work. Whether it's designing a dress or building an application, I love creating things that inspire and engage. I'm eager to combine my skills in tech and fashion to make a meaningful impact.
