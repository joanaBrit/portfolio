Say Hello!

My background as a Fashion Design is perhaps an unconventional one in software development. I first applied to the course with the encouragement of my friends and family, but was not entirely sure if it was for me. However, as I learned more, I discovered a new passion in coding. I found a new spark of curiosity and joy as I discovered the same opportunities for experimenting, problem-solving, continuous learning, and being creative as I did in fashion. I am excited to embark on this journey as a Software Engineer. I'm eager to further develop my abilities, expand my knowledge, and embrace the challenge of achieving what I once considered impossible.
