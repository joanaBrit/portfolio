About Me

Say Hello!

Hi! I'm Joana. I'm a junior software engineer based in London. I'm positive and resilient, have a passion for learning and thrive on tackling new challenges.

In 2023, I made the career transition from fashion to technology, through General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive bootcamp. I gained hands-on experience developing full-stack web apps, both solo and in teams, with React and Node.

I continue to improve every day! I'm an active open-source contributor to Women Coding Community, and completed a new personal project using TypeScript and Material UI. I recently obtained the JavaScript MOOC Sprint certificate from Code First Girls, and am now admitted to the Cajigo 100 Women in Tech Accelerator.

I love creating - whether it's in fashion or through code. My background in design brings a unique perspective to my work in engineering. I'm eager to combine both worlds and put my skills into action!
